### Key Learnings

#### Design Thinking

- **Business-first approach**: Always start from the business domain, not technology.
- **Ubiquitous language**: The importance of a common vocabulary between business and tech teams.
- **Bounded contexts**: How to identify and manage complexity in large systems.

#### Technical Architecture

- **Event storming technique**: A practical method for modeling business processes.
- Use **Event-driven communication** instead of synchronous calls.
- **Integration patterns**: Understand when to use sync, async, pub/sub, streaming.
- **Compute spectrum**: Criteria for choosing from VM → containers → serverless.

#### Modernization Strategy

- **Phased approach**: Do not rush; ensure there is a clear roadmap.
- **7Rs framework**: Various paths available depending on the characteristics of each application.
- **ROI measurement**: Cost reduction + business agility.
